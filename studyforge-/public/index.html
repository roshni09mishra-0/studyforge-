How it works

1) Upload

Select a PDF, DOCX, or TXT. StudyForge extracts text automatically.

2) Generate

AI produces summaries, quizzes, flashcards, and a study plan.

3) Track

Your results sync to Firebase so you can continue on any device.

Security & privacy

Firebase Auth + Firestore rules

Your account is protected by Firebase Authentication. Data is stored per-user in Firestore and restricted with security rules. You control what you upload and what you keep in your library.
